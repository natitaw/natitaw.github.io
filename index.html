I am a data analyst living and working at a health startup called Diet Doctor in the wonderful city of Stockholm. Our purpose at Diet Doctor is to help people live a healthy and fulfilling life. Currently we are doing that by providing world-class educational content, meal plans and recopies. My role is to make sure that the purpose of the company is fulfilled. As a data analyst, I do that by setting up the necessary frameworks and mindset shifts required to ensure that the company is data driven at the product and company level.

I am a highly curious and open individual, driven to uncover the why behind phenomenon I encounter in life. I consider myself to be someone who is not afraid of confronting the most basic assumptions. I have a knack for abstract thought and I am always keen on engaging in philosophical discussions. I am a person who has deep respect to those who I consider to be my teachers regardless of what I am pursuing, I believe this to be due to my natural interest in spiritual philosophy. I am usually found reading a book or tinkering on my laptop.
